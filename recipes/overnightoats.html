Overnight Oats

There easy and quickOvernight Oats are going to be your favourite breakfast for meal preping. Prepare them in 10 minutes and have them ready in the morning!

Ingredients

  • 30g of oats
  • 15g of protein powder
  • almond milk
  • fruit of choice (we used berries)
  • tsp of chia seeds

Steps

  1. Mix the oats, protein powder, milk and chia seeds in a jar.
  2. Set in the fridge overnight.
  3. Take them out of the fridge in the morning
  4. Add your fruits.
  5. (You can also add nut butter)
