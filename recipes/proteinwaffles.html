Protein Waffles

These waffles packed of protein are going to be your new favourite breakfast! All ingredients are natural except for the protein powder (except when you are using a clean one). If you have just a few ingredients you can make them in 10 minutes right at home!

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • half banana
  • 20g of buckwheat flour
  • 15g of protein powder
  • milk (of choice)

Steps

  1. Mix the wet ingredients.
  2. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients.
  3. Spray your waffle maker with some oil spray.
  4. Put in the waffle maker for around 2 minutes.
  5. After being done serve with your favourite toppings.
